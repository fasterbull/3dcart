A few years ago the government came to us with the need for a reliable and truly portable safe. After testing a number of designs with the help and assistance with their agents, we created the Console Vault® Security Briefcase. A product that offers incredible portability without compromising security, the Security Briefcase is one of our most prized offerings for government personnel and others with high-security needs.

The entire body of the Console Vault® Security Briefcase is made of 16-gage plate steel with welded seams and hem enforcement on the lid, providing simply the best in security. The interior and lid of the Security Briefcase is lined with high-density convoluted cell foam, which keeps your belongings in place while being transported.

The Console Vault® Security Briefcase also comes with a 36" vinyl-coated steel cable that enables carriers to loop and secure the Security Briefcase to another object for enhanced security measures. Rated at 4,200 pounds and including a nylon storage case, the cable is one of the Security Briefcase's most prized features.

The Security Briefcase features Console Vault®'s Barrel Key Lock and comes with two sets of keys. Console Vault®'s premium lock, the Barrel Key Lock features a case-hardened body that makes it nearly impossible to drill out. The Security Briefcase also features a solid molded handle complete with a nylon enforcement and the case itself is finished with a commercial-grade silver vein that provides exceptional durability and a lifetime of use.

Used on a daily basis by hundreds of local and federal law agencies, the Security Briefcase is the perfect solution for those requiring the utmost in security for the transporting of weapons, sensitive documents, and evidence.


 

Features Console Vault®'s Triple Guard Lock System
Console Vault® has recently made the greatest design improvement since the product was first developed. Our new Triple Guard Lock System incorporates a latch design similar to the pins used in the doors of bank vaults, an overall improvement that greatly increases security.

The design also includes a front channel that incorporates the lid into the body of the vault itself, ultimately eliminating stress from the physical lock. The end result: the most secure product Console Vault® has ever made.
